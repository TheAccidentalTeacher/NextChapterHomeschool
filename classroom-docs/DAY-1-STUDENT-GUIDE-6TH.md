## Step 0 - Log In (before rounds start)

1. Go to **bit.ly/SomersCiv**
2. Sign in with your **lowercase** name and your password
3. You land on your **team dashboard** - check that it shows:
   - Your **team name**
   - Your **role(s)** - if you have two roles, a purple banner tells you both
   - Your **teammates** as colored chips at the top

> If anything looks wrong, raise your hand NOW - before rounds start.

## How Every Round Works (the same pattern repeats 4 times)

Each round follows the exact same steps. Only the **lead role** and **resource earned** change.

| Round | Lead Role | Resource Earned |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| BUILD | Architect | Production |
| EXPAND | Merchant | Reach |
| DEFINE | Diplomat + Lorekeeper | Legacy |
| DEFEND | Warlord | Resilience |

**Every student submits every round** - not just the lead. If you hold two roles, you submit **twice** (once per role).

Here is what a single round looks like:

### During the Round (8 minutes - 6 min for DEFEND)

1. **A decision card appears** on your screen with:
   - A short historical context (gray box at the top - read it!)
   - A question your civilization is facing
   - 2-4 answer options (buttons you tap to select)
2. **Read the question out loud to your team** - discuss it quickly
3. **Pick an option** - tap the button for your choice (it highlights blue)
4. **Write your justification** - in the text box below the options:
   - A sentence-starter hint is shown to help you get going
   - You need **at least 2 sentences** (the counter shows 0/2, 1/2, 2/2)
   - The border turns **green** when you have written enough
5. **Hit the blue Submit Decision button** at the bottom
6. **You will see a green checkmark Submitted confirmation** - now wait for your teammates

> The timer is real. If time runs out before you submit, your answer does not count. Do not wait until the last second.

> Two-role students: After you submit for one role, a second card appears for your other role. Submit that one too!

### After the Round - Routing (2 minutes)

The **lead role** for that round gets a routing screen. They decide where the earned resources go. Everyone else waits.

- BUILD round - **Architect** routes Production
- EXPAND round - **Merchant** routes Reach
- DEFINE round - **Diplomat** routes Legacy
- DEFEND round - **Warlord** routes Resilience

> If you are not the lead, just sit tight. Your teacher will advance to the next round when routing is done.

---

## Round 1 - BUILD (8 minutes + 2 min routing)

**Lead role: Architect** - Earns: Production

**What the Architect does:**
- You are the star of this round - the question is about what to build, where to settle, or how to develop your land
- Read the question to your team, pick an option, write your justification, submit
- After the round ends, **you route the Production** your team earned

**What the Merchant does:**
- A decision card appears for you too - answer the question from your perspective
- Pick an option, write 2+ sentences, submit

**What the Diplomat does:**
- Same thing - you get a card, pick an option, justify it, submit

**What the Lorekeeper does:**
- Same thing - answer the question, write 2+ sentences, submit

**What the Warlord does:**
- Same thing - answer the question, write 2+ sentences, submit

> Remember: If you hold two roles (like Architect + Lorekeeper), you submit **two separate cards** this round.

---

## Round 2 - EXPAND (8 minutes + 2 min routing)

**Lead role: Merchant** - Earns: Reach

**What the Merchant does:**
- This is your round - the question is about trade, exploration, or extending your civ's influence
- Read the question, discuss with your team, pick an option, justify, submit
- After the round, **you route the Reach** your team earned

**What everyone else does:**
- You each get a decision card - pick your option, write 2+ sentences, submit before time runs out

> Same pattern as BUILD. Read - Discuss - Pick - Write - Submit.

---

## Round 3 - DEFINE (8 minutes + 2 min routing)

**Lead role: Diplomat + Lorekeeper** - Earns: Legacy

**What the Diplomat does:**
- This is your round - the question is about culture, identity, alliances, or your civ's values
- Read the question, discuss, pick, justify, submit
- After the round, **you route the Legacy** your team earned

**What the Lorekeeper does:**
- This is also your round - the question may ask about recording history, traditions, or cultural identity
- Answer your card, write 2+ sentences, submit

**What everyone else does:**
- Same as always - get your card, answer it, submit

---

## Round 4 - DEFEND (6 minutes + 2 min routing)

**Lead role: Warlord** - Earns: Resilience

> This round is **shorter - only 6 minutes**, not 8. Move fast!

**What the Warlord does:**
- This is your round - the question is about defending territory, responding to threats, or protecting your people
- Read, discuss, pick, justify, submit
- After the round, **you route the Resilience** your team earned

**What everyone else does:**
- Get your card, answer it, write 2+ sentences, submit - fast! Shorter timer!

---

## After All 4 Rounds - Resolve and Exit

Your teacher handles this part. The game calculates results - how your decisions affected your civ's resources, territory, and standing. You will see an updated dashboard.

Then we are done for today. One full epoch complete.

---

## The Golden Rules

1. **Talk to your team.** Every decision should be a team decision.
2. **Submit before the timer runs out.** Late submissions do not count.
3. **Every person submits their own card.** Do not submit for someone else.
4. **Two roles = two submissions.** If you hold two roles, submit both cards.
5. **Raise your hand** if something looks broken - do not just sit there confused.
6. **Today is practice.** Experiment. Make mistakes. Learn.

---

## What To Do If...

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|-----------|
| I do not see a decision card | Raise your hand - your teacher may need to advance the round |
| I submitted but my teammate did not | Encourage them, but do not touch their screen |
| I have two roles but only see one card | Submit the first one - the second card appears after |
| The timer ran out before I submitted | It is okay today - tell your teacher so they know |
| I do not understand the question | Ask your teammates first, then raise your hand |
| My screen says Waiting for teammates | You are done! Sit tight until the round advances |
