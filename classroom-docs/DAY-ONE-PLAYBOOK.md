# DAY-ONE-PLAYBOOK.md — ClassCiv Epoch 1, Full Class Period
### What every person sees, every step, in order.
> **URL:** https://bit.ly/mrsomersciv  
> **Grade 6:** 5 teams of 3 (Architect · Merchant · Diplomat)  
> **Grade 7/8:** 6 teams of 3–4 (Architect · Merchant · Diplomat · Lorekeeper on Teams 1 & 4)

## PRE-CLASS (Before students arrive)

### You (DM)
1. Open your laptop: go to **https://bit.ly/mrsomersciv** → log in → `/dm`
2. You see two game cards: *Classroom Civ — 6th Grade 2025-26* and *Classroom Civ — 7th & 8th Grade 2025-26*
3. Click **Manage** on the relevant period's game → you land on `/dm/game/[id]`
4. You see:
   - **DMControlBar** at top: `Epoch 1 · Login & Recap` · Pause · Advance · Autoplay
   - **Submissions panel** (right side): empty — no students in yet
   - **Map** (main area): world map, your 5–6 team territories colored, sub-zones visible
5. Click **📺 Projector** button on the game card → opens `/projector?game_id=...` in a new tab
6. Drag that tab to your classroom TV / second screen

### Projector (TV)
- Showing: **Civilization Standings board** (all 5–6 teams listed, all resources at 0)
- No login required. It auto-found the active game.
- Press **M** or click map icon to toggle between leaderboard and map view

### Students
Not here yet. Accounts exist. Game exists. Waiting.

## STEP 1 — LOGIN & RECAP (`login` step, ~2 min)

### You (DM)
- Game is at `Login & Recap` step. **Don't advance yet.** Let students settle.
- Watch the Submissions panel — it won't show submissions during login but you'll see your teams loading in

### Each Student
1. MacBook open → go to **https://bit.ly/mrsomersciv**
2. Landing page: big **ClassCiv** title, two buttons — "Student / Teacher Login" and "Projector View"
3. Click **Student / Teacher Login** → Clerk sign-in page
4. Type their first name (lowercase) in both username and password fields → Sign In
5. Lands on `/dashboard`

**First-time only — Civilization Naming Prompt:**
- A modal appears: *"What will your civilization be called?"*
- One student per team types the name — all three teammates will see the same prompt until one submits
- Once named, the civ name appears in the top bar for all teammates and on the projector leaderboard

**After naming — what each student sees on their dashboard:**

| Role | What they see |
|---|---|
| **Architect** | 🏗️ Architect – Build panel. Production: 0. No buildings yet. PurchaseMenu locked (no production to spend). |
| **Merchant** | 🧭 Merchant – Expand panel. Reach: 0. Trade board empty. All trade actions locked until Expand round. |
| **Diplomat** | 📜 Diplomat – Define panel. No alliances. No laws. War Exhaustion: 0. Actions locked until Define round. |
| **Lorekeeper** (7/8 only) | 📖 Lorekeeper panel. Codex empty. No writing tech yet. Locked until Define round. |

**Top bar (all students):** Team name · Civ name (once named) · Epoch 1 · Login & Recap · Food 0 · Production 0 · Reach 0 · Resilience 0

**LoginRecapCard:** Shows *"Previously on your civilization..."* — Epoch 1 has no history yet, so this is a brief welcome/flavor text generated by Claude Haiku.

**Four tabs available:** Action (default) · Map · Tech · Role

### Projector (TV)
- Civilization names start populating the leaderboard as teams submit them
- Resources all still 0 — that's correct and expected
- Good time to show the map view so students can see their territories

## STEP 2 — BUILD ROUND (`build` step, 8 min / 6th; 6 min / 7–8)

### You (DM)
- Click **▶ Advance** in DMControlBar → step moves to `BUILD Round`
- Top bar now shows: `Epoch 1 · BUILD Round`
- **Submissions panel** activates — you'll see each team's 5 roles with empty circles. They fill as students submit.
- Watch for submissions coming in. You can click any submission to read the justification and approve/override if needed.
- Optional: fire a **Global Event** from the Tools tab — but not recommended for Day 1. Let them find their footing.

### Each Student — Architect (BUILD round leader)
- Their **Action tab** is now live: the round submission card appears
- **Historical context shown:** a flavor paragraph about their region/era (from the question bank)
- **Question prompt:** something like *"Your settlement needs shelter. Which material do you prioritize?"* with 3–4 options
- They select an answer, write a **justification (minimum 2 sentences)** — the field counts sentences and shows a check when valid
- Hit **Submit**
- After submit: green confirmation card. Their answer is locked. They can now browse the **Map** or **Tech** tabs.
- **PurchaseMenu** also available: if they have production from a prior epoch they could spend it here — Epoch 1 they have nothing, so the menu shows 0 production

### Each Student — Merchant (BUILD round)
- Action tab shows: *Waiting for BUILD round to complete.* Merchant is not the BUILD lead.
- They see their resources (all 0 still) and can browse Map and Tech tabs
- Can spectate the map to identify neighboring teams for future trade

### Each Student — Diplomat (BUILD round)
- Same as Merchant — waiting state. No actions required this round.
- Good time to look at the map and identify potential alliance targets

### Each Student — Lorekeeper (7/8 only, BUILD round)
- Waiting state. Browsing codex (empty). No action required.

### Projector (TV)
- Leaderboard shows teams, resources still 0 (production not routed yet)
- If you toggle to Map: territories visible, no unit markers yet
- No event overlays unless you fired one

## STEP 8 — DEFEND ROUND (`defend` step, 6 min both grades)

### You (DM)
- Click **▶ Advance** → `DEFEND Round`
- **This is the most volatile round.** Watch for conflict flags.
- If any two teams are in adjacent sub-zones with soldiers, a **ConflictFlagBanner** may appear at the top of your DM page — bright red warning
- You resolve conflicts manually: read both teams' justifications, decide who wins, override if needed via SubmissionOverrideModal
- **Note for Day 1:** No units exist yet, no conflicts will fire. DEFEND round is slow on Day 1.

### Each Student — Warlord
- Wait — **Warlord role doesn't exist in this roster.** Your teams have Architect · Merchant · Diplomat (plus Lorekeeper for some 7/8 teams). There is no Warlord assigned.
- DEFEND round is a no-submission round for Day 1 since no one holds the Warlord role.
- *(This is intentional for the alpha — if you want Warlord in play, it needs to be assigned in setup-classes.ts)*

### All Students (DEFEND round, Day 1)
- Waiting state. All submissions already in for their roles.
- Free browse time: Map, Tech tree exploration.

### Projector (TV)

## STEP 10 — RESOLVE (`resolve` step, DM-triggered, no timer)

### You (DM)
- This step **does not auto-advance.** You are in control.
- The game pauses and waits for you to trigger resolution.
- **What you do here:**
  1. Read through all team submissions in the Submissions panel
  2. Approve any that need manual review (most auto-approve)
  3. Optional: fire a **Global Event** that will affect next epoch (famine, trade boom, barbarian raid)
  4. Optional: send **Intel Drops** — secret messages to individual teams ("rumor has it Team 3 is hoarding grain")
  5. Click **📺 Push to Projector** → triggers the ResolveSequence overlay on the TV
- **Projector during RESOLVE:** Full-screen animated resolution sequence — teams' resource totals, who grew, who struggled, epoch summary. Students watch the TV.
- Click **▶ Advance** when resolution is complete → moves to `Exit Hook`

### Each Student (RESOLVE)
- Their screens are now showing the resolve/waiting state
- Eyes on the projector TV — that's the show right now

### Projector (TV)
- **ResolveSequence overlay** plays: animated reveal of each team's epoch results
- Population changes, resource totals, territory updates
- This is the cinematic moment — let it run

## WHAT COULD GO WRONG ON DAY 1

| Problem | What you'll see | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Student can't log in | Clerk sign-in error | Double-check: username = first name lowercase, no spaces. `ejay` not `e.jay`. |
| Student sees "not assigned to a team" | Dashboard shows empty state message | Run `npx tsx scripts/setup-classes.ts` again — likely a Clerk user ID mismatch |
| Projector shows wrong game | Leaderboard shows different team names | Pass `?game_id=...` explicitly in the URL — get it from the DM game card URL |
| Submissions not appearing in DM panel | Panel stays empty | Refresh. Polls every 5 sec. If still empty, check browser console for 401. |
| Civ naming prompt won't dismiss | Student stuck on naming screen | Another teammate may have already named the civ — refresh the page |
| RESOLVE doesn't fire the sequence | Projector stays on leaderboard | Click "Push to Projector" button explicitly from the Tools tab in DM |
| Question bank shows generic text | Submission card shows placeholder | Question bank may not be seeded — run the generation script |
